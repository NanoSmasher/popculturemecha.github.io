Welcome all, to the wonderful world of J-Music!  I've been a fan since my early anime days and in an effort to spread my love for Japan's musical contributions, I've compiled a list of various artists and bands to whet ones musical appetite.  This is by no means an exhaustive list, but is instead a subjective one, made up of artists I listen to and enjoy.  However, this list will get updated with time, as I hear new catchy tunes.  Each artist/band will be getting a post as well, in which I will survey some of that artist/band's work, for your perusal.  Have fun!

(Pictures with a red border have posts completed for them.  As well, I am fully aware that there may be groups on this page that are not Japanese.  It's not that important.)
