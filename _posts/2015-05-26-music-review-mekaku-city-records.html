This is a pretty old album, but I came across it again by chance recently and thought it would be nice to do a quick review.  If you're not a fan of Vocaloid, then this probably isn't for you.  However, there's always a first time, and I would suggest trying this out if you feel like giving it a chance.

Mekakucity Records is a Vocaloid album put out by Shizen no Teki-P, with songs done by IA, the Vocaloid developed using the Japanese female singer Lia.  The songs are attributed to Jin.  This album consists of 13 tracks, 11 songs and 2 sound bites.
