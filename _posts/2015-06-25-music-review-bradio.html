This is gonna be a short one, as BRADIO has only been around for about 5 years, and they don't have much out.  If you are familiar with BRADIO, it's probably because of the opening they did for Death Parade, "Flyers".  Let's take a listen.

[embed]https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9wh8FgsEtNQ[/embed]

To me, BRADIO's music is like a party in progress.  It's very funky with a touch of jazz and a traditional J-Rock feel.  Here's another hit of theirs.  From their 2013 mini-album Diamond Pops, "Golden Liar".

[embed]https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=saOq9S8mfSc[/embed]

BRADIO hasn't changed much in the time they've been together, so that should be good enough.  I just wanted to feature them quickly so I can keep the posts somewhat regular.  Check out more at BRADIOweb.  Have a great day!
