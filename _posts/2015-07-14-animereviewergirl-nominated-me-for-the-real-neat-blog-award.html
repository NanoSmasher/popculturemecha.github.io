Well, let's do this, I guess.  Thanks for the nomination animereviewergirl.  Check out her stuff, it's pretty good.

To reiterate, here are the rules for this thing:

1. Put the award logo in your Blog

2. Answer 7 questions asked by the person who nominated you

3. Thank the people who nominated you, linking their blogs

4. Nominate any number of bloggers you like, linking their blogs

5. Let them know you nominated them (by commenting on their blog)

6. Ask the bloggers 7 new questions

animereviewergirl's questions:

1. What is your least favorite anime?

Good question.  I think my least favorite anime is probably Gun-dou Musashi.  It's... just... so bad.

2. What anime character would be your rival?

Tough question, but I'd probably say Sunohara from Clannad.  We have similar attention-grabbing tendencies, so we'd probably compete to see who's the funniest.

3. What is your favorite Pokemon?

Psyduck.

4. Do you have a talent?

Not really, I'm sort of a jack-of-few-trades, master of none.

5. Favorite genre of music?

I don't really have a genre I lean towards, I just go through phases where I like one type of music and then I move on.

6. What is your favorite Disney movie?

There are way too many to choose from, but for now let's say both Fantasia and Fantasia 2000.

7. What is your favorite dessert?

The Sahara, obviously.  Oh, wait... ice cream.  Orange sherbert especially, but I'll eat just about any flavor.
