For those who are need aid in their efforts to emulate my blogging supremacy, here is a tip:

Always read your stuff before posting it.  It's a matter of understanding that people will get annoyed during the reading of your stuff if a word is spelled wrong or a sentence sounds weird.  Just doing a quick read through eliminates 99.99% of bacteria- I mean clerical errors, and that can massively increase readability.
